- **Link:** https://jai.scs.stanford.edu/ | https://github.com/stanford-scs/jai (inferred)
- **HN:** 445 points, 262 comments — #7 on front page
- **What:** A lightweight sandbox that wraps AI coding agents (Claude Code, Codex, etc.) with copy-on-write filesystem isolation. One command (`jai claude`) gives the agent full access to your working directory while keeping the rest of your home dir behind an overlay.
