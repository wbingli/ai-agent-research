1. **Agents Aren’t Coworkers — Embed Them in Your Software**  
   Source: Hacker News

4. **Hyper-DERP: C++/io_uring DERP relay — same throughput as Tailscale’s derper, half the cores**  
   Source: Lobste.rs  
   URL: https://hyper-derp.dev/blog/hyper-derp-announcement/  
   A practical backend/networking performance story: reimplementing a DERP relay around C++ and `io_uring` to cut CPU usage while preserving throughput. Useful for anyone thinking about relay-heavy systems, real-time connectivity, edge infrastructure, or high-concurrency service design.

5. **Lambda Calculus Benchmark for AI**  
   Source: Hacker News  
   URL: https://victortaelin.github.io/lambench/  
   A compact benchmark aimed at evaluating whether models can reason over lambda calculus rather than simply pattern-match. Interesting because it probes symbolic/programming reasoning in a way that may expose weaknesses hidden by broader coding benchmarks.
